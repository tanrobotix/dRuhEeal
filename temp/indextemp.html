To Nguyen Duy Tan

CEO & Founder

Một deverloper chân chính. Một người giàu tình cảm.

If the sky symbolizes the dreams, grand plans and a holistic overview so comprehensive insight of oneseft… then the daily anxieties dragged our eyes down on the ground, to sink in a bunch of people hustle-busters, to deal with the obstacles ahead and then we have no time to launch the eye up far away to the sky, to see how our dreams could be grand are... And that is when we forget what’s really important: it is the dream. Everyone has their own dreams. Maybe it’s just the ordinary demands of daily living. Or it may also be the longing for longer plans for oneseft. So that, I’m no an exception, I have a lot of my own dreams and aspirations. Every dream, whether casual or greater, all need to be fueled by enthusiasm, determination and relentless efforts of youth. Youth is a very precious stuff that not everybody knows and used correctly. It is a priceless gift that the god endowed us on the way to conquer our dreams. There is no time better than now to begin the implementation of our dreams. I want to become a CEO in the technology sector. I knew it was a path full of thorns and difficulties. But keep in mind that the situation is changing, not like the paper-based, so I need to have the necessary changes to achieve the next step or choose the other path better for implementing my goal. I don’t let the failure make me down, use those failure to make it become goal desired. Learn to accept the criticisms, the advice from friends around, learn to listening, to filtering out the good things, bad things, self contemplating whether to hear the advice or forget them. And you know? We should sacrifice if necessary. You will have motivation to make that sacrifice useful. Remove any obstacles on the path of implementation. Create a list of things that it holding me back, possibly my friends could help me to do this. Because, I maybe didn’t realize that wasting time on some unnecessary things could made me unable to work out my abilities. Put away the excuses, sometimes someone complained, we got mistakes by something objective come to, but we have to learn to accept it, shouldn’t take it as a justification for the mistakes. Let thank to it cause we have the experiences for the next encounter to resolve better. I need to believe in myself and my own skills, I relize that I need self discipline, patience, consistency, keeping commitments. If you want to make your dreams come true, the first thing you need to do is wake up. On the way to success, there is no trace of lazy men.
